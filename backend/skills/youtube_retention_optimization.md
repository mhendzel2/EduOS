# YouTube Retention Optimization

## Analytical Strategies
1. **The 30-Second Hook**:
   - First frame must directly visually pose the specific friction-point or mystery explicitly referenced in the Thumbnail. Absolutely no generic slow intro logs or branding title cards.
2. **Re-engagement Pacing**:
   - Visually cycle framing lengths, angles, or diagram colors every 5-7 seconds. 
   - Narrative pacing must introduce a "J-Curve" mini-payoff every 45 seconds maintaining dopamine continuity.
3. **Meta-Data Chaptering**:
   - Ensure explicitly typed markers. Keywords inserted into chapter titles must bridge to highest-search volume strings (e.g. "Phase Separation basics", "What is Hi-C Chromatin").
